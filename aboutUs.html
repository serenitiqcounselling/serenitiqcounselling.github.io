About Us

Our Mission

At Serenitiq Counselling, our mission is to provide compassionate, accessible, and effective mental health support to individuals in need. We strive to create a safe and supportive environment where clients can openly discuss their challenges and work towards recovery. Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their mental health journey and live fulfilling lives.

  • Compassionate care
  • Accessible services
  • Effective mental health support

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading provider of mental health services, known for our commitment to excellence and our dedication to the well-being of our clients. We aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health and create a community where individuals feel supported and understood.

  • Excellence in mental health services
  • Breaking the stigma
  • Community support and understanding

Our Values

Our core values guide everything we do at Serenitiq Counselling. We believe in treating every individual with respect, dignity, and empathy. We are committed to providing high-quality services that are client-centered and evidence-based. Our values include:

  • Respect and dignity
  • Empathy and compassion
  • Client-centered care
  • Evidence-based practices

Meet Our Team

Dr. Samantha Wilson

Dr. Samantha Wilson is the founder and lead therapist at Serenitiq Counselling. With over 15 years of experience in the mental health field, she is dedicated to providing compassionate and effective support to her clients. Dr. Wilson specializes in individual and group therapy, as well as crisis intervention.

Michael Thompson, LMFT

Michael Thompson is a licensed marriage and family therapist with a focus on couples and family therapy. He is passionate about helping families improve communication and resolve conflicts. Michael has been with Serenitiq Counselling for over 10 years.

Emily Johnson, LCSW

Emily Johnson is a licensed clinical social worker who specializes in child and adolescent therapy. She uses a variety of age-appropriate techniques to help young individuals navigate their emotions and develop healthy coping skills. Emily has a background in school social work and has been a valuable member of our team for 8 years.

David Lee, PhD

Dr. David Lee is a clinical psychologist with a specialization in cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT). He works with clients to address a wide range of mental health issues, including anxiety, depression, and trauma. Dr. Lee is dedicated to helping clients achieve their personal goals and improve their quality of life.
